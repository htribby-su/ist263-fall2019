Activities Off Campus

The New York State Fair

The New York State Fair occurs every fall right here in Syracuse!

The fair includes agriculture, museums, concerts, rides, and of course lots of fried food.

Syracuse University usually runs a shuttle to and from the fair, and there is a weekend where college students get in for only $1!

This is a great place to go and have a fun night with your friends.

Apple Picking

Apple picking is a classic fall activity in New York.

There are many different orchards nearby campus, and the university usually provides a free trip for first year students!

Visit a farm near you to pick lots of apples, drink some warm apple cider, and even get an apple cider donut!

Green Lakes State Park

Green Lakes State Park is located about 20 minutes away from Syracuse University.

The park includes a golf course, beaches, boat rentals, picnic areas, and much more.

This is a great place to go to enjoy the final summer days before the notorious Syracuse winter arrives!

Central New York Regional Market

The CNY Regional Market is a local farmers market located right by the train station and Destiny USA mall.

This market is open year-round on Saturday and Sunday from 7am to 2pm.

This is a great place to come and get some fresh food and to get to know your local neighbors!
